What is AUTOBUS?

AUTOBUS (Autonomous Business System) is a scalable, multi-agent framework that enables rapid, end-to-end deployment of business capabilities—powered by human expertise and intelligent agents. It combines LLMs, traditional software tools, and structured workflows to automate and enhance complex cross-functional operations at a fraction of the cost.

About Color Chalk AI
